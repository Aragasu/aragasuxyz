This bot is designed to be simple, clean, and easy to use. It is currently in an early testing stage, so features are still being improved and expanded.

Unlike many large giveaway bots that are packed with unnecessary features, this bot focuses on doing giveaways well while staying lightweight and easy to understand.

⚡ Why this bot?

  • Clean and minimal giveaway system
  • Button-based entry (one click to join)
  • Fast and lightweight
  • Reroll winners anytime
  • End giveaways early
  • Simple commands and easy setup

🚧 Early Testing

This bot is still in early development, so you might see new features and improvements frequently. However, the goal is to keep it cleaner and simpler than many other giveaway bots while still being powerful enough for most servers.

Perfect for servers that want a straightforward giveaway system without unnecessary complexity. 🎉
